Privacy Policy

PagePen - Web Annotator & Screenshot  |  Last updated: April 17, 2026

Summary: PagePen does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything runs locally in your browser.

1. Data Collection

PagePen does not collect any personal information. We do not collect:

  • Names, emails, or contact information
  • Browsing history or page content
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies or identifiers

2. Local Storage

PagePen uses the browser's built-in window.localStorage (scoped to the current page's origin) solely to save your preferences such as:

  • Toolbar position (floating, pinned left, pinned right)
  • Default drawing settings (color, stroke width, opacity)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

3. Permissions

PagePen requests the following browser permissions:

  • activeTab: To overlay the drawing canvas on the current webpage when you click the extension icon
  • scripting: To inject the drawing tools into the active tab
  • downloads: To save annotated screenshots as PNG files to your device when you click the Save button

4. Screenshots

When you use the screenshot feature, the captured image is processed entirely within your browser and saved directly to your device. No image data is uploaded or shared.

5. Third-Party Services

PagePen does not use any third-party services, APIs, analytics, or advertising platforms.

6. Changes

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us at the email listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.
